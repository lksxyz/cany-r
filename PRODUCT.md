# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Travelers across Indonesia (Bali, Jakarta, Yogyakarta, Lombok, and beyond) who end their trip with leftover foreign cash that traditional money changers won't accept. They want to convert small amounts of physical cash into USDC easily, instantly, and safely.

Local agents vetted by the platform who facilitate the exchange and earn a margin.

## Product Purpose

0verice turns leftover travel cash into USDC via verified local agents, escrow-protected on Monad. No more losing value in unused foreign currency. No more money changers refusing small amounts. Instant settlement, any amount accepted.

## Brand Personality

- Ceria (Cheerful): warm, friendly, approachable — not cold or corporate
- Aman (Safe): trustworthy, secure, escrow-protected — confidence is key
- Sederhana (Simple): frictionless, minimal steps, clear UX — travel should be easy

Voice: warm Indonesian hospitality meets modern crypto convenience. Indonesian-English bilingual. Friendly but precise.

## Anti-references

- Too crypto: avoid dark mode + neon green grids, avoid Matrix/cyberpunk aesthetics, avoid "blockchain bro" tone
- Too banking: avoid navy blue + gold, avoid sterile corporate layouts, avoid bureaucratic language
- Too generic SaaS: avoid the cream-white-blue startup template

## Design Principles

1. **Tropical warmth**: Let the Bali setting breathe through the design. Warm colors, organic shapes, natural textures.
2. **Confidence through clarity**: Trust comes from transparency. Show exactly what happens at each step — escrow, confirmation, settlement.
3. **Lightness**: Both visually (light tones, airy spacing) and experientially (fewer steps, less friction).
4. **Mobile-first**: Travelers use phones. Every screen works beautifully on small screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG 2.1 AA. Good contrast on warm palettes. No critical functionality tied to color alone.
